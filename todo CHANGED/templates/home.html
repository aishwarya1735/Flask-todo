TO-DO LIST

Creating a list of tasks helps to clarify exactly what needs to be done. By breaking down larger tasks into smaller, more manageable ones, it becomes easier to focus and prioritize. Having a to-do list can help you stay focused on the tasks at hand. With a clear idea of what needs to be done, you can avoid distractions and stay on track..
